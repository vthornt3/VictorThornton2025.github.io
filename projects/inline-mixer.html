Inline Lipid-Reagent Mixer to Prevent Line Crystallization

Engineered an inline mixing + gentle recirculation module with a short heated section to keep a lipid-based transfection reagent homogeneous—eliminating crystal buildup and line clogs in automated runs.

Overview

Automated transfection workflows were failing intermittently due to reagent crystallization in low-flow segments and dwell zones, causing pressure spikes and clogged tubing. I designed an inline mixer cartridge that maintains homogeneity, minimizes nucleation sites, and drops into existing plumbing without disrupting throughput.

My Role

  • Lead for root-cause analysis (pressure/flow logging, dwell mapping, temp correlation).
  • Mechanical design of a quick-swap static-mixer cartridge with localized heat jacket.
  • Test planning & execution: soak tests, multi-day reliability, pressure-drop characterization.
  • Created BOM, drawings, build guide, cleaning SOP, and operator troubleshooting tree.

Problem → Approach → Outcome

Problem: Crystallization and deposition after short dwell times and cold spots in the line → clogs, aborted runs, and high MTTR.

Approach: Instrumented the line with in-line pressure and flow logs; correlated failure events with dwell, temperature, and shear. Prototyped a compact static-mixer geometry (tri-clamp housing) to avoid clogging.

Outcome: Pilot runs showed minimal clog events and faster recoveries. Operators could run more experiments without interruptions.

Design Highlights

  • Static-mixer core: tuned element pitch to balance mixing vs. pressure drop.
  • Cleanability: smooth internal radii; compatible with cleaning SOP.
  • Repeatable by design: clear prints, wide tolerance stack, and no exotic processes—so you can produce “N more” units on short notice.

Validation & Results

  • Clog event rate: ↓ by ~94% vs. baseline over 10 multi-day runs.
  • Run success rate: ↑ by ~20%; aborted runs reduced from 18% to 1.2%.

Troubleshooting & Ops

  • “If-this-then-that” triage card for operators (pressure spike, flow stall, temp drift).
  • Pre-flight checks added to reduce setup errors (air in line).
  • Uptime dashboard tags mixer swaps and correlates with downstream performance.

Artifacts

  • CAD, drawings, and BOM for mixer cartridge + heat jacket.
  • Build guide, cleaning SOP, and operator decision tree.
