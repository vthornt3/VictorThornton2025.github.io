Jaw-Fixation Gripper for Image-Guided Jaw Realignment Surgery

Designed a sterilizable, high-stability jaw holder (“claw”) for a surgical robot performing osteotomy and realignment.

Overview

Image-guided craniofacial surgery needs rigid fixation with minimal occlusal interference and fast setup.

My role

  • End-effector design (constraints, quick-release, sterilization)
  • FEA + tolerance stack-ups
  • Benchtop jigs and ergonomics checks

Approach → Outcome

Compared cam-lock vs. wedge vs. screw-clamp concepts; added textured pads & guided alignment. V1 met holding-force and repeatability targets in benchtop tests; V2 focuses on faster actuation and re-sterilization ease.

Highlights

  • Materials: Ti-6Al-4V/PEEK options; ISO 10993 & sterilization routes
  • Anti-slip geometry; single-handed lock; radiolucent features where needed
  • HFE review for gloved operation & visibility
